The Reality on Our Campus

57% of graduate students are food insecure. Over half our members cannot reliably access nutritious food.

28% skip meals to save money. 7% have gone entire days without eating.

The Proposed Solution

The Solution: A Student Levy for Food Security

We propose a small student levy - $1.50 to $5.00 per term - to create a dedicated Food Security Fund. This is less than the cost of a single coffee, but together it would generate sustainable funding to address the crisis we just outlined.

If approved by members, levy collection starts September 2026 to fund:

  • Emergency food relief for students in crisis
  • Subsidized healthy meal programs
  • Kitchen infrastructure for student-run food initiatives
  • Cultural food programs for international students
  • Partnerships with local food providers

This would transform our ad-hoc, underfunded response into a comprehensive, sustainable program that matches the scale of the problem.
